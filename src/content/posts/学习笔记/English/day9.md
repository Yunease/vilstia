# 正文

## 课程进度：阅读专项课-【课时一】上

**定位词**，在文章中并不是每篇文章都是有用的，可以先阅读题目，找出题干中的关键词，例如人名，年份，破折号等。

需要注意的是，很多时候会出现同义替换，如important → vital；change → alter；need → require



例题：Eric Schmidt is one of the bosses of Google, which owns AlphaGo. He thinks AI will be positive for humans. He said people will be the winner, whatever the outcome. Advances in AI will make human beings smarter, more able and "just better human beings."

1. What does Eric Schmidt think of artificial intelligence ?

   A) It will be far superior to human beings.

   B) It will keep improving as time goes by.

   C) It will prove to be an asset to human beings.

   D) It will be here to stay whatever the outcome.



在这个题中，关键词是 `Eric Schmidt` 和 `artificial intelligence` 



例题：The companies developing self‑driving vehicles should be partnering with state and federal authorities to offer retraining for this massive workforce, many of whom will be displaced by the new technology. This is similar to what's happening in the coal and oil industries, a situation that fuels much of the current political discontent in this country.

1. What is the result of the introduction of new technologies in energy industries ?

   A) Political dissatisfaction.

   B) Retraining of employees.

   C) Fossil fuel conservation.

   D) Business restructuring.



在这个题中，关键词是 `new technologies` 和 `energy industries`
